fusion law

Sep 26, 2023

教科书链接：https://www.cambridge.org/core/books/algorithm-design-with-haskell/824BE0319E3762CE8BA5B1D91EEA3F52

一些重要的fusion law：

  • map 
    map f . map g = map (f . g)
  • concatMap after map 
    concatMap f . map g = concatMap (f . g)
  • foldr after map 
    foldr f e . map g = foldr (f . g) e
  • foldr: 对于所有的非无限长list xs, 如果有对于任意 x , y 成立： 
    h (f x y) = g x (h y)

    ，则有

    h (foldr f e xs) = foldr g (h e) xs

    证明：

    • 如果xs为空： 
      h (foldr f e [])
= h e
= foldr g (h e) []
    • 如果xs不为空且假设foldr fusion law 在xs上正确： 
      h (foldr f e (x:xs))
= h (f x (foldr f e xs))
= g x (h (foldr f e xs))
= g x (foldr g (h e) xs)
= foldr g (h e) (x:xs)
  • foldl: 对于任意有限长的list xs, 如果对于任意 x , y 成立 
    h (f y x) = g (h y) x

    ,则有

    h (foldl f e xs) = foldl g (h e) xs

    证明：

    • 如果xs为空 
      h (foldl f e [])
= h e
= foldl g (h e) []
    • 如果xs不为空 
      h (foldl f e (x:xs))
= h (f (foldl f e xs) x)
= g (h (foldl f e xs)) x
= g (foldl g (h e) xs) x
= foldl g (h e) x:xs

还有其它一些fusion law

  • scan: 
    map (foldl f a) . inits = scanl f a
  • filter map : 
    filter p . map f = map f . filter (p . f)
